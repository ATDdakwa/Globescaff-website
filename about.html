Our Vision

To be a leading commodity broking company in Zimbabwe and sub-Saharan Africa, respected for the quality of our products and services, exceptional customer service and for the way we conduct our business.

Our Mission

•To deliver strong returns to our shareholders by providing high-quality products and services at affordable prices, backed by excellent customer service.

•To take part and contribute to Zimbabwe’s economic growth by being a facilitator and key strategic partner to key players within the mining, construction and agricultural sectors.

•To help Zimbabwe and other African countries towards self-reliance and self-sufficiency in the production and sourcing of critical and basic commodities, by partnering with significant players in the market and being a preferred supplier across multiple sectors and industries.

•To lead the way in areas such as innovation, sustainability and environmental conservation and to set a good example for other companies to follow

Our Values

• Customer Service: As a world-class organisation, we understand that we exist to serve and satisfy our customers. Accordingly, our customer orientation reflects approachability, dependability and continuous learning.

• Efficiency: We recognise that efficiency leads to better productivity and profitability therefore it is at the heart of our service offering . We understand that our customers and stakeholders need to make decisions on a timely basis and our service proposition seeks to enable efficiencies throughout our entire value chain.

• Trust & Transparency: We strive to be open and honest in all that we do, both internally and externally, building trust throughout the organization.

•Leadership: We thrive on being leaders in our business, markets and communities. To drive this we focus on continuous improvement, partnership and professionalism.
